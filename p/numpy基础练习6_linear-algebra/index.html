Q13. Compute the sign and natural logarithm of the determinant of x.

x = np.arange(1, 5).reshape((2, 2))
sign, logdet = np.linalg.slogdet(x)
det = np.linalg.det(x)
assert sign == np.sign(det)
assert logdet == np.log(np.abs(det))
print sign, logdet
